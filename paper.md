## Abstract

This repository provides a structured LaTeX/Overleaf dissertation template
designed for Master in Data Science (MDS) students under SMSTU academic
guidelines. The template simplifies thesis preparation by offering predefined
chapters, formatting standards, and citation support, enabling students to
focus on research quality rather than document structure.

## Keywords
LaTeX, Dissertation Template, Academic Writing, MDS, SMSTU

## Summary
This paper presents a LaTeX dissertation template designed for Master's in Data
Science students under IOST SMSTU guidelines. The template standardizes academic
formatting, improves reproducibility, and reduces overhead for students.

## Statement of Need
Many postgraduate students struggle with LaTeX formatting rather than research
content. This template addresses that gap by providing a ready-to-use structure.

## Design and Features
The template includes predefined chapters, citation management, and compatibility
with Overleaf and local LaTeX builds.

## Usage
The template is publicly available on GitHub and can be reused under the MIT License.
